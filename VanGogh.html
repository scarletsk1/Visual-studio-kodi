Van Gogh’s Vision: Color and Emotion Exhibition

Discover the profound world of Vincent van Gogh at Van Gogh’s Vision: Color and Emotion, an extraordinary showcase running from March 5 to March 19, 2025. Witness the intensity of his palette and the emotional depth of his brushstrokes like never before.

This exhibition brings together iconic works and intimate sketches, providing a glimpse into Van Gogh’s innovative techniques and personal struggles. Interactive experiences and in-depth analyses reveal the profound connection between his art and his life.

Perfect for art lovers and curious minds alike, this event offers a heartfelt journey through the colors and emotions of one of history's most beloved artists. Experience Van Gogh’s brilliance and feel the passion behind every stroke.

Dates: March 5 – March 19, 2025
Daily: 15.00–18.00
Location: Room 23
The Alexander Grant Cultural Hall
Tickets: Adults from £9, Members and under-16s free.
