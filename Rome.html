The Walls of Rome: A Journey Through Time.

Experience the grandeur and history of ancient Rome in the immersive event, "The Walls of Rome: A Journey Through Time." Step into the world of Roman fortifications as you explore the architectural marvels that defined an empire and protected its glory for centuries.

This event brings to life the stories of power, resilience, and ingenuity behind Rome's legendary walls. Through interactive displays, virtual reconstructions, and rare artifacts, discover how these formidable structures influenced the city’s development and safeguarded its legacy.

Whether you’re an avid historian or a curious adventurer, this unique experience offers a fresh perspective on the engineering feats and cultural significance of Rome’s ancient walls. Don’t miss this opportunity to walk through history and uncover the secrets of an empire.

Dates: 26 September 2024 – 23 February 2025
Daily: 10.00–17.00 (Fridays 21.00)
Location: Room 37
The Joseph Hotung Great Court Gallery
Tickets: Adults from £10, Members and under-16s free.
