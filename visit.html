Getting Here

A bus departs from both Durrës and Tirana, making it easy to visit the museum from these nearby cities. For detailed bus schedules, visit Transport Schedule.

The museum offers a spacious parking area free of charge. Whether you arrive by car or bus, parking is hassle-free and convenient.
