Picasso Unveiled: Art Beyond Boundaries

Immerse yourself in the genius of Pablo Picasso at "Picasso Unveiled: Art Beyond Boundaries." This extraordinary event invites you to explore the revolutionary works of one of history's most iconic artists, delving into the techniques and visions that reshaped modern art.

Experience the bold colors, striking forms, and evocative narratives of Picasso's masterpieces through curated displays and insightful interpretations. Then, unleash your creativity in an exclusive painting class inspired by Picasso’s style, where you’ll learn to channel his unique approach to form and abstraction.

Perfect for art lovers and aspiring creatives alike, this event offers a rare chance to connect with Picasso’s legacy and express your own artistic vision. Don’t miss this inspiring celebration of innovation and self-expression.

Dates: 7 November 2024 – 30 March 2025
Daily: 10.00–17.00 (Saturdays 20.30)
Location: Hall 2
The Evelyn Harcourt Heritage Gallery
Tickets: Adults from £25, Members and under-16s free.
